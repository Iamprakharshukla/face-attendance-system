⚙️ CONFIGURATION OPTIONS
═════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Face Recognition
  • FACE_RECOGNITION_MODEL = 'hog' or 'cnn'
  • FACE_DISTANCE_THRESHOLD = 0.6 (adjust for accuracy)

Camera & Upload
  • MAX_CONTENT_LENGTH = 500MB
  • FACE_RECOGNITION_MODEL selection
  • Upload folder paths

Email (in .env)
  • MAIL_SERVER
  • MAIL_PORT
  • MAIL_USERNAME
  • MAIL_PASSWORD
  • ORGANIZATION_EMAIL

Session & Security
  • SECRET_KEY (change for production)
  • SESSION_COOKIE_SECURE
  • SESSION_COOKIE_HTTPONLY
  • DEBUG mode
